HOW: Register Yourself ?

1. Click on "Register: Locate the "Register" button on the homepage and click on it to initiate the registration process.

2. Fill out Registration Form: You will be directed to a registration form.

3. Agree to Terms and Conditions: Read through the terms and conditions of using the platform and, if you agree, check the box indicating your acceptance.

4. Verify Email: After submitting the registration form, you will receive a verification email at the email address you provided. Click on the verification link in the email to verify your account.

5. Click on “Log in”: Once your email is verified, you will be redirected to the website where you can log in using your registered email address and password.

6. Upload Project: Once logged in, navigate to the "Upload Project" section.
